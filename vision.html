Vision Eye Hospital

Brand Identity & Print Design Case Study

Project Overview

Developed a clean and trustworthy visual identity for a specialized eye care hospital. The goal was to communicate professionalism, clarity and medical reliability.

Design Strategy

  • Minimal logo combining letterform and eye symbolism
  • Blue & green palette to convey trust and healthcare credibility
  • Clean typography for readability and accessibility
  • Consistent brand application across print materials

Deliverables

  • Logo System
  • Visiting Card
  • Billboard Design
  • Tri-Fold Brochure
